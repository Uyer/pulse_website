ARTISTAS

FB SOUNDCLOUD Juno

Based in Berlin since 2012, you might have caught her playing at many clubs in town, such as Hoppetosse, Kater Blau, Sisyphos, Chalet, mostly at Club der Visionäre, where she hosts together with Ernesto Ferreyra the Loosen Up events, and at Golden Gate, where she has a monthly residency. Her exquisite taste and signature technique have also traveled far and wide from Avant Garten Buenos Aires to Chapeau Rouge Prague, from Off Sonar Barcelona to Cocoon Punta del Este and from Badaboum Paris to Quinto Sol Festival Berlin and Santiago de Chile.

In the last years Ema has seen her productions signed to the likes of Savor, Ama Recordings, Get Slow, Drumma, Arms & Legs, Cachai, Perception Dub, Bla Bla Music, Raw Trax, but it is her EP “Nature of Time” on her own label Astelagüel which has caught the attention of renowned international artists.
